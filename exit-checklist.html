Guest Departure Checklist

Thank you for staying with us! Please follow these steps for a smooth checkout.

Back to Guest Info

General

  • Remove all personal belongings (check cupboards, drawers, under beds, bathrooms).
  • Close and latch all windows and sliding doors.
  • Return any moved furniture to its original position (including on the balcony).
  • Give the apartment a "broom clean" tidy-up.

Kitchen & Dining

  • Put your last load of dishes in the dishwasher and turn it on.
  • Clean, dry and put away any kitchenware that is not going into the dishwasher.
  • Wipe down kitchen benches, stove, and the dining table.
  • Empty the fridge and freezer of any perishable food items.
  • Take away your rubbish and recycling to the designated areas.

Laundry & Bathrooms

  • Place used bathroom and beach towels in the main bathroom's shower or bath.
  • Leave used beds unmade (we will strip them).
  • If heavily soiled, a quick wipe of bathroom surfaces is appreciated.
  • Put the clothes drying rack away (do not leave it on the balcony).

Outdoor Areas

  • Bring in the cushions from the outdoor chairs.
  • If you used our bicycles, please make sure they are locked up back in the garage where you found them.

Final Check

  • Have you left any USB chargers, clothes or food behind?
  • Switch off air-conditioning, lights and all appliances.
  • Send a quick message to the host to confirm departure (optional but appreciated).

Locking Up

  • Remove your car from the garage first! Park it out front or in the visitors parking area.
  • Leave all keys/fobs in the basket on the cupboard in the hallway.
  • Lock and pull the door shut behind you when you leave. You do not need a fob to use the lift to get back down.
