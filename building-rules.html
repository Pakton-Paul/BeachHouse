Building Rules

These are the complex rules to ensure a safe and pleasant stay for everyone.

A copy of these rules is shown on the wall inside the lift.

Back to Guest Info

Basement Access

When driving in or out of the basement, turn your headlights on and go slow. To maintain security, after you pass through the gate, please wait for it to close behind you before driving away.

Visitor Parking

The outside car parks are for visitors only. Residents and guests should only use them for short periods â€” otherwise, please park in your allocated basement space. (By-law 16)

Garbage Chute

No glass is to be placed in the chute. Please ensure all your rubbish is securely bagged before disposal.

Recycling

The large yellow-top recycling bins are in the B1 and B2 foyers. No plastic bags, no polystyrene, and no food scraps are to be placed in these bins.

Lift Etiquette

Please do not hold the lift doors open for extended periods as it impacts its operation for other residents.

Pets

Any pets must be carried while in the lift and through all common areas. Please manage your pets to prevent noise that may disturb other residents. (By-law 18)

Bicycles

Bicycles are not permitted above the Lower Ground (LG) floor. They must enter and exit via the Basement (B1) or Lower Ground (LG) levels. (By-law 25)

Washing on Balconies

Washing, towels, and clothing must not be hung on balconies where they are visible from the street. (By-law 8)

Shopping Trolleys

If you use the building's trolleys, please return them to their pickup point in the basement in a timely manner.

Pool Area

No glass bottles or containers of any kind are permitted in the pool enclosure. Swimming hours are between 7:00 AM and 9:00 PM.
