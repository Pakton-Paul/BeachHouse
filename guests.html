How Things Work

Arrival & Departure

  • Check-in Time: From 2:00 PM
  • Check-out Time: By 11:00 AM

Late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. Please ask us in advance.

Access - Keys, Lift & Garage

You have been provided with two sets of keys.

  • The small black fobs opens the front door, and grants access to Floor 1 (this apartment) and B1 (the carpark) in the lift.
  • The larger grey fob opens the main garage door. Entry to the garage is via Sutton Street.
  • Your allocated secure parking spot is #7.

Pet Policy

We have a strict no-pet policy. However, requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis and require our specific approval for each booking.

Internet

Wireless internet is provided via the 5G router located in the broom cupboard. The data allowance is large but not unlimited. Please refrain from very large downloads.

  • Network: Unit_7
  • Password: flowerfold

Smart TV

The TV provides free-to-air channels. You can also connect your own devices or log in to streaming apps.

  • Use the provided HDMI cable to connect laptops.
  • Access apps like YouTube and Netflix using your own account. Please remember to log out before you leave.

Air Conditioning

The apartment has a ducted air-conditioning system for your comfort.

  • The system can take a few minutes to start up.
  • Please be sensible with temperature settings; extremely low settings can cause condensation.
  • Important: Please turn the system off when opening balcony doors or when you leave the apartment.

Appliances

On departure, please place your last load of dishes in the dishwasher and start the cycle.

  • Dishwasher tablets are located under the sink.
  • For detailed instructions, you can view the user manual.
View Dishwasher Manual

Garbage Disposal

  • The garbage disposal chute is through the door to the left of the lift on level 1. Please ensure all garbage is double-bagged before disposal.
  • A recycling bin is located near the lift in the carpark (B1).

Seasonal & Local Events

Redcliffe Markets: Every Sunday from 8am to 2pm, Redcliffe Parade comes alive with one of the best markets in the region.

Whale Watching (June - October): During the winter months, you can book a whale watching cruise. Tours leave directly from the Redcliffe Jetty and can be booked at the office in Comino's Arcade on the waterfront.
